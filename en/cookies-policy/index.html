What cookies we use, why and how to manage them.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites store on your device when you visit them. They allow the site to remember your preferences and collect statistical information about how the site is used.

2. Cookies we use

Technical cookies (always active)

Strictly necessary for the website to function. No consent required.

  • cookieConsent: Stores your cookie consent choice. Duration: 1 year.

Analytics cookies (require consent)

Allow us to understand how users navigate the site to improve content and functionality. Only activated if you accept cookies.

  • _ga, _ga_*: Google Analytics 4. Used to distinguish unique users and analyse browsing behaviour. Duration: 2 years / 1 minute.

This site uses Google Analytics with IP anonymisation and without remarketing or audience segmentation features.

3. Managing cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

This policy may be updated in response to regulatory changes or changes in the services we use. We recommend reviewing it periodically.
